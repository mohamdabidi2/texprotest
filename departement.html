OUR DEPARTMENTS

Creative Lab

Our Creative Lab is where innovation meets craftsmanship. Our talented team combines technical skills with creativity for continuous research and collection development. In this dynamic environment, designers collaborate closely with all departments to bring concepts to life.

The Creative Lab oversees the entire development process from initial concept to final product:

  • Style Definition: Creating unique designs that align with current trends while maintaining our brand identity
  • Fabric Selection: Sourcing and selecting the highest quality materials that meet our sustainability standards
  • Pattern Development: Creating precise patterns that ensure perfect fit and comfort
  • Prototype Creation: Developing initial samples to test design concepts before production

Cutting Department

Our Cutting Department represents the crucial first step in the garment manufacturing process. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology including precision cutting machines, meter counters, and advanced lighting systems, this department ensures accuracy and efficiency in every cut.

We continuously invest in the latest cutting technology to maintain the highest standards of precision and quality. Our skilled technicians are trained to maximize fabric utilization while minimizing waste, supporting our commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices.

The department features:

  • Computer-aided design and cutting systems
  • Automated fabric spreading machines
  • Precision cutting tools for various fabric types

Sewing Department

The heart of our production process lies in our Sewing Department, where skilled artisans transform cut pieces into finished garments. With decades of collective experience, our sewing team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create products of exceptional quality.

Our sewing lines are organized for maximum efficiency while maintaining meticulous attention to detail. Each garment passes through multiple quality checkpoints throughout the sewing process to ensure perfect construction.

The department is equipped with:

  • Specialized machines for various stitch types and fabric weights
  • Ergonomic workstations designed for comfort and efficiency
  • Real-time production monitoring systems

Washing and Dyeing Department

Our Washing and Dyeing Department represents our commitment to innovation and sustainability. We've invested in a completely new sustainable laundry facility that meets the highest environmental standards while delivering superior finishing results.

The department features a closed-loop water system where 70% of water is recycled and reused in production. Our innovative process saves up to 90% of water compared to traditional methods. As a testament to the purity of our recycled water, we maintain an aquarium within the facility where fish thrive in our treated water.

Our sustainable washing and dyeing technologies include:

  • Laser Washing: Reduces water usage while increasing fabric strength by 50%
  • Ozone Technology: Replaces traditional finishing processes, dramatically reducing chemical usage
  • Water Recycling System: Advanced filtration that purifies water for reuse
